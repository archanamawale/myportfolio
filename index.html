ANNOUNCEMENTS

Help Guide

OVERVIEW:
The announcements feature is introduced to aid users stay informed with crucial updates, information and warnings related to the Group Directory application, ensuring you never miss a beat. This platform, only visible to the administrators, allows to create, edit, and remove announcements promptly, ensuring that our users are always up-to-date with the latest development activities- maintainence, patching, implementing changes, and more.

KEY FEATURES:
  • Create Announcements with ease: Announcements with the defined type, visibility and active durations.
  • Flexibility to choose visibility Options: At Global level or Specific page
  • Comprehensive Overview: All the announcements at-a-glance, both active and expired.
  • Streamlined Communication: All the users around the globe will get notified with just creating an announcement
WORKFLOW:

Creating Announcements:

To create an announcement, click and fill in the mandatory details in the new pop-up window.
Title: To add the title to the announcement.
Short Content: To display about the announcement in brief
Content: To let the users know about the announcement in detail. However, this is not a mandatory field.
Type: This specifies the type of announcements whether informative or warning.
Start Date & End Date: This defines the duration of an announcement.

Updating Announcements:

Once the announcement is created, in the tabular view, click on to edit the announcement. After making the desired updates, save the updates.

Viewing Announcements:

To see how the announcement will be visible to the intended users, click on

Deleting Announcements:

The announcements that are no longer required or added mistakenly can be deleted by clicking on .
Please note that once the announcements are deleted cannot be recovered.



VISIBILITY CONDITIONS:
  • At present, the amount of active announcements banners are limited to three at a time.
  • Announcements will be visible based on the duration set on them and will be labelled as follows.
    Active: If the present date falls between the start date and the end date of the announcement.
    Upcoming: If the start date of the announcement is in near future.
    Expired: If the end date of the announcement has passed.
  • You can opt for either of the visibility options for the announcement banners to be displayed:
    Global: This will display the announcement within the whole application.
    Single Page: This will limit the announcement to a specific page.

DATES & TIMEZONES:
Announcements are intially timestamped in your default timezone i.e., you will be able to set the start date and end date of the announcement as per your timezone. However, you will be able to see the announcement's equivalent date and time adjusted in CET timezone just below the respective fields so as to ensure clarity, consistency, and fostering coordination accross different locations around the globe.

ANNOUNCEMENT BANNERS:
  • Announcements will show up at the top of the page as per the visibility set on it whether Global or Single Page.
  • The colour of the banners specifies the type of announcements-  Info  or  Warning 
  • To view the content in detail, click on Read More... on the announcement banner displayed on the top of the page.
  • To hide the announcement banner, click on on the top right corner of the banner. However, you can log in to new browsing session or via incognito mode (Chrome)/ inPrivate window (Edge), if you wish to recheck the announcement.
